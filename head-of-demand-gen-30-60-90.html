Context & Mission

What you're walking into — and what success looks like.

The Opportunity

Maybern is the fund accounting platform for private equity. We sell $150K–$1.6M ACV enterprise deals into PE firms with complex, consultative sales cycles. Today our pipeline is sourced almost entirely through outbound and network. Your mandate is to build the demand gen function from scratch — creating repeatable, measurable inbound pipeline that compounds over time.

01

Days 1–30: Learn

Immerse in the market, the buyer, the product, and the current motion. Earn context before building.

1–30
Phase 1 Learn & Diagnose
Market & Buyer Immersion
  • Shadow 5+ discovery calls and demos with AEs (Bryan, Gabrielle, Brad, Will, Asaf) — understand how deals actually happen
  • Study 3–5 closed-won and 3–5 closed-lost deals end-to-end; map the buying committee and decision triggers
  • Build a working understanding of fund accounting, PE waterfalls, NAV calculations, and the "status quo" competitor (spreadsheets + outsourced admin)
  • Interview 3–5 existing clients on what drove their decision and what content/events would have been helpful earlier
Internal Alignment
  • Weekly 1:1s with Riley (SVP Sales), Matt Arnold (Rev Ops), Chris Salvati (Sales Manager), Tiffany Simonsen (Solutions Engineering), and Ross (CEO) — understand priorities, pain points, and what's been tried before
  • Audit existing content library, website, and any paid/organic channels currently running
  • Map the current lead-to-opportunity handoff process; identify where leads drop or stall
  • Review CRM data with Matt to understand pipeline sources, conversion rates, and velocity by channel
Competitive & Market Landscape
  • Catalog competitor marketing motions — who's running events, publishing content, buying keywords
  • Identify the 5–10 industry conferences, communities, and publications where PE ops leaders spend time
  • Assess the SEO/SEM landscape for fund accounting, PE operations, and adjacent keywords
Service Industry & Channel Partner Strategy
  • Map the PE services ecosystem and develop a marketing strategy for each channel: fund formation law firms, fund accounting auditors, technology partnerships, and fund technology consultants
  • Understand how each group currently refers or influences technology decisions at PE firms — where does Maybern fit in their workflow and recommendation patterns?
  • Identify co-marketing opportunities (joint webinars, co-branded content, referral programs, conference co-sponsorship) and prioritize by pipeline potential
  • Deliver a Service Industry Marketing Playbook outlining positioning, messaging, and activation plan for each partner category
SEO, Content & Website Audit
  • Complete a full SEO audit — technical health, domain authority, keyword rankings, backlink profile, and content gaps vs. competitors. Use AI tooling to accelerate this; there's no reason it takes more than a week
  • Conduct a comprehensive content audit — catalog every existing asset (blog posts, whitepapers, case studies, landing pages, decks), assess quality, map to buyer journey stages, and flag gaps
  • Audit the website through a customer journey lens — walk the site as each buyer persona (Fund CFO, COO, Controller, Head of Ops) and evaluate: Is the value prop clear in 5 seconds? Can they self-educate? Are CTAs aligned to where they are in the buying process? Where do we lose them?
  • Deliver a combined SEO + Content + Website Audit Report with prioritized recommendations and quick wins
Product Fluency — Mock Demo
  • By Day 25–30, deliver a mock product demo to Riley and Tiffany Simonsen (Solutions Engineering)
  • Demo should cover: core fund accounting workflows, waterfall mechanics, investor reporting, and Maybern's key differentiators vs. status quo
  • This is not about perfection — it's about proving you understand the product deeply enough to create campaigns that speak the buyer's language, not generic marketing speak
Day 30 Deliverables
  • Market & Buyer Landscape Memo (written summary of what you learned)
  • SEO + Content + Website Customer Journey Audit Report
  • Service Industry Marketing Playbook (law firms, auditors, tech partners, consultants)
  • Current State Audit — inventory of all channels, content, conversion data
  • Opportunity Map — where the biggest gaps and quick wins are
  • Mock product demo delivered to Riley + Tiffany
  • Draft 90-Day Demand Gen Strategy (presented to Riley + Ross for alignment)
02

Days 31–60: Build

Lay the infrastructure, launch first programs, and start generating signal. Prioritize systems that compound.

31–60
Phase 2 Build & Launch
Demand Gen Infrastructure
  • Restructure the signals data stack (RB2B, Clay, etc.) in partnership with Riley — audit current data flows, clean up routing logic, and ensure intent signals are actually reaching AEs in a usable format
  • Build attribution and reporting dashboards — pipeline sourced, pipeline influenced, cost per opportunity, channel velocity
  • Set up or optimize marketing automation (email nurture sequences, behavioral triggers, lifecycle stages)
  • Establish a weekly demand gen staff meeting run by Matt Arnold (Rev Ops) — cross-functional alignment on campaign performance, pipeline attribution, and handoff quality across Sales, Demand Gen, and SDRs
First Programs Live
  • Launch 1–2 high-value content assets targeting Fund CFOs (e.g., benchmark report, transformation playbook, ROI calculator)
  • Stand up a targeted digital campaign (LinkedIn, potentially Google) focused on top-of-funnel awareness with PE operations leaders
  • Design and plan Maybern's first owned event or webinar series — "Office of the Modern CFO" roundtable or similar
  • Activate an ABM pilot targeting 20–30 named accounts from the sales team's priority list
Content & Brand Foundation
  • Develop a content calendar for the next quarter tied to buyer journey stages and deal themes
  • Create 2–3 case study or customer proof points in collaboration with CS/Sales
  • Establish Maybern's point of view in the market — what do we believe that our buyers don't yet know?
  • Stand up an executive LinkedIn ghostwriting motion — identify 2–3 internal voices (Ross, Riley, and/or subject matter experts), develop a posting cadence, build a content bank of POV-driven posts, and establish a lightweight approval workflow so posts go out consistently
Day 60 Deliverables
  • Signals data stack (RB2B, Clay, etc.) restructured and routing to AEs
  • Weekly demand gen staff meeting running with Rev Ops
  • Attribution dashboard tracking pipeline by source and influence
  • First content asset published and promoted
  • ABM pilot live with account-level engagement tracking
  • Q3 content calendar finalized
  • Executive LinkedIn ghostwriting motion live with first posts published
03

Days 61–90: Execute & Optimize

Drive measurable pipeline, iterate on what's working, and present the forward plan.

61–90
Phase 3 Execute & Scale
Pipeline Generation
  • Deliver first batch of marketing-sourced qualified opportunities to the sales team
  • Run weekly pipeline review with Riley and Matt — which channels are converting, where to double down, what to kill
  • Optimize campaigns based on 30 days of performance data — adjust targeting, messaging, creative, and spend
  • Launch second wave of ABM plays informed by pilot learnings
Sales–Marketing Flywheel
  • Establish a regular content-from-sales loop: deal themes, objections, and competitive intel feed the content engine
  • Build SDR enablement materials — sequences, talk tracks, and follow-up content aligned to demand gen campaigns
  • Co-develop with Sales a "warm handoff" process for event and webinar attendees
Scale Planning
  • Present a 6-month demand gen roadmap with budget, headcount, and pipeline targets to leadership
  • Identify what vendor, agency, or contractor support is needed vs. what stays in-house
  • Propose event strategy for the next 2 quarters — owned events, sponsorships, and conference presence
  • Recommend martech stack additions or changes based on 60 days of hands-on experience
Day 90 Deliverables
  • $1.5–2.5M in marketing-sourced qualified pipeline created and tracked
  • Marketing-influenced pipeline identified, tagged, and reported
  • Performance report: what's working, what's not, and why
  • 6-month demand gen roadmap with budget and headcount proposal
  • Event strategy and calendar for next 2 quarters
  • Martech recommendation memo

North Star
